# Nau Mai ki Zynthio
## Language: Te Reo Māori
## Duration: 50 seconds
## Avatar tone: warm, respectful, grounded
## Brand: Zynthio

[GESTURE: warm smile, nod]

Kia ora. Nau mai, haere mai ki *Zynthio*.

[PAUSE 1s]

Kei te hanga mātou i tētahi mea rerekē — he pūnaha auaha mō ngā ringatoi motuhake, ngā kaiwhakaputa puoro, me ngā kaihanga pēnei i a koe.

[GESTURE: open hand]

Whakaarohia pēnei. Kotahi te pūnaha — whitu ngā waitohu — ko ngā mea katoa e hiahiatia ana e koe ki te *waihanga*, ki te *ako*, ki te *tiaki*, me te *whakatipu* i ō mahi. Kāore he kaiwhakawā. Kāore he ārai.

[PAUSE 0.5s]

Mai i te waihanga puoro AI me *SongPal* — ki te mātauranga me *MOSOKO* — ki te tiakitanga āhua hinengaro me *KERVALON* — ka hono katoa. Ā, nōu katoa.

[GESTURE: nod]

Nō Aotearoa mātou, i whakatūria e Corey McIvor. E whakapono ana mātou ki tētahi mea nui — ko te *mana auaha*.

[PAUSE 1s]

Tō toi. Ō taputapu. Ō tikanga.

[GESTURE: confident smile]

Kia hanga tahi tātou. Nau mai ki te pūnaha.
