## Overview

SongPal is the flagship product of the Zynthio ecosystem — an AI-powered audio production and music collaboration platform. It gives independent artists access to professional-grade music creation tools, powered by CoreeyAI and integrated with Suno API for generative audio capabilities.

SongPal is the product where the entire Zynthio stack becomes tangible: the AI intelligence of CoreeyAI, the creative output of DJ Zynrose, and the educational context of MOSOKO all converge here.

---

## Mission

To be the most creator-friendly AI music production platform — giving independent artists the tools to produce, arrange, and collaborate at a professional level, without needing a studio or a label.
