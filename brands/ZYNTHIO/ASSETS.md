## Usage Guidelines

1. Do not stretch or distort the logo
2. Maintain minimum clear space of 1× the logo height on all sides
3. Do not place the logo on backgrounds that reduce contrast below WCAG AA
4. Use Signal Gold sparingly — it is an accent, not a fill colour
5. All brand assets are copyright ZYNTHIO / Corey McIvor
