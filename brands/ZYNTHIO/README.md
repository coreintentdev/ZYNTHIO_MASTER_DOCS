## Overview

ZYNTHIO is the parent company and umbrella brand for a vertically integrated creative-technology ecosystem. It governs seven interconnected brands spanning AI engineering, music production, education, legal protection, and developer tooling.

ZYNTHIO is not a single product — it is an architecture for creative sovereignty: the infrastructure, intelligence, and culture that independent creators need to build, distribute, and protect original work on their own terms.

---

## Mission

To give independent creators a complete sovereign stack — from AI tooling to legal protection — so they can create, distribute, and own their work without dependence on extractive legacy platforms.
