## Colour Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Earth Brown | `#5C3A1E` | Warmth, grounding |
| Harvest Gold | `#E8A020` | Energy, optimism, CTAs |
| Cream | `#FAF3E0` | Backgrounds, breathing room |
| Forest Green | `#2D6A4F` | Growth, progress indicators |
| Charcoal | `#2B2B2B` | Body text |

*Palette rationale: MOSOKO evokes craft, warmth, and growth — distinct from the more technical CoreeyAI palette.*

## Usage Guidelines

- MOSOKO assets should feel approachable and warm — not corporate or cold
- Use Harvest Gold for progress indicators and achievement moments
- Course completion graphics should use the full palette, not just black/white
