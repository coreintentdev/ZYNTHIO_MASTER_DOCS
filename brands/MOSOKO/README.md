## Overview

MOSOKO is the education and pedagogy brand of the Zynthio ecosystem. It teaches independent artists, students, and emerging creators how to use the full Zynthio stack — from AI-assisted music production with SongPal to understanding IP, licensing, and creative business fundamentals.

MOSOKO is not a generic music school. It is built for creators who want to own their entire pipeline: conception, production, distribution, and protection.

---

## Mission

To make the knowledge of professional music production, AI tooling, and creative business ownership accessible to independent creators worldwide — with a curriculum rooted in real tools and real outcomes.
