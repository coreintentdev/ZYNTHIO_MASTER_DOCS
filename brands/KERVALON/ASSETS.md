## Voice & Tone

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Authoritative | Speaks with the weight of the law — calmly |
| Precise | Legal language when required; plain English when possible |
| Protective | The stance is always: we protect what you build |
| Measured | No hyperbole. Accurate claims only. |

---

## Usage Guidelines

- KERVALON branding should not appear in consumer-facing marketing for SongPal or MOSOKO
- Use in: legal documents, compliance reports, partnership agreements, TM filings
- The seal/icon is reserved for official documents only
- Do not use Justice Red as a background colour — accent only
