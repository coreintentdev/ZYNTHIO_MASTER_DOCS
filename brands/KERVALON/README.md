## Overview

KERVALON is the legal and intellectual property arm of the Zynthio ecosystem. It manages trademark filings, copyright strategy, compliance obligations, and partnership contracts for all seven Zynthio brands.

KERVALON exists because IP protection is not optional for a creator-tech ecosystem — it is foundational. Without protected IP, the entire stack is vulnerable. KERVALON makes sure that what Zynthio builds, Zynthio owns.

---

## Mission

To provide robust, proactive IP and legal infrastructure that protects every brand, product, and creative work in the Zynthio ecosystem — and to make that same infrastructure accessible to independent artists through KERVALON services.
