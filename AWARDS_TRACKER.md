## Entry Strategy

**Priority order:**
1. **Free or low-cost entries** — maximise exposure without burning runway
2. **Competitions with non-dilutive capital** — grants, prizes, credits
3. **Accelerators with strong networks** — where equity exchange is worth the access
4. **Recognition awards** — credibility for fundraising and partnership conversations

**Best-fit categories for Zynthio:**
- AI / Machine Learning startups
- Music technology / Creative technology
- Fintech (for the trading/risk management component)
- Solo founder / Bootstrapped startup
- NZ / ANZ startups
- Open source projects
