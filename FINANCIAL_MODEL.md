## Key Assumptions

1. SongPal free tier converts at 10–15% to paid (industry benchmark: 5–15% for music SaaS)
2. MOSOKO cohorts fill based on DJ Zynrose audience + organic content — no paid acquisition needed initially
3. CoreeyAI B2B API clients sourced through direct founder network outreach
4. Founder (Corey McIvor) does not draw salary in Year 1; compensated via equity
5. gTrade (trading bot) provides supplemental self-funding; not included in projections
6. NZD/USD exchange rate assumed ~0.60 for international comparisons
7. Market growth assumptions: music production software +8% YoY, AI music +25% YoY
