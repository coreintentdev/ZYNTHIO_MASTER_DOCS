## Brand Story

### The Short Version (50 words)

Zynthio is a sovereign AI ecosystem built in New Zealand by Corey McIvor — a developer, AI engineer, and musician who decided that independent creators and traders deserve institutional-grade tools without institutional gatekeeping. Seven brands, one stack, one founder. No filler. All signal.

### The Medium Version (150 words)

Corey McIvor is a New Zealand/Australian developer and independent music producer who got tired of the same story: AI tools that are opaque, expensive, and designed to extract value from the people who use them. So he built his own stack.

Zynthio is a vertically integrated AI ecosystem — seven interconnected brands spanning autonomous trading (CoreIntent), multi-model AI orchestration (CoreeyAI), music production (SongPal), education (MOSOKO), legal/IP protection (KERVALON), and a music artist persona (DJ Zynrose) that proves the tools work in public. All of it is engineered in-house, deployed on sovereign infrastructure, and incorporating in New Zealand.

The trading engine runs on a competition-based model — it profits when it performs, not from subscriptions. The same AI layer powers music composition and education. One engineering investment, multiple verticals. Early stage, real infrastructure, globally ambitious.

### The Long Version (300 words)

Most AI platforms follow the same playbook: wrap an API, charge a subscription, collect data, repeat. The user doesn't own anything. The platform profits whether the user wins or loses. Corey McIvor decided that wasn't good enough.

McIvor is a full-stack developer, AI engineer, and independent music producer — a New Zealand/Australian citizen currently based in Managua, Nicaragua. He builds every system in the Zynthio ecosystem himself: the multi-model AI orchestration layer (CoreeyAI), the autonomous risk-managed trading engine (gTrade via CoreIntent), the AI music production platform (SongPal, trademark filed with IPONZ), the education brand (MOSOKO), and the legal/IP infrastructure (KERVALON). He also releases music under the artist name DJ Zynrose — making him both the builder and the user of his own tools.

The core innovation is structural. CoreIntent's trading engine uses a competition-based model — performance is the product, not subscriptions. This creates genuine alignment between the platform and its users. Under the hood, CoreeyAI orchestrates across Claude (Anthropic), Grok (xAI), and Perplexity — routing each task to the optimal AI model rather than depending on a single vendor.

Zynthio is incorporating as ZYNTHIO LIMITED under the New Zealand Companies Act 1993. The SongPal trademark is filed with IPONZ (#1318588). Production infrastructure is live on a sovereign VPS running Docker and Python 3.11. The codebase and documentation are public on GitHub.

The ecosystem addresses a combined market opportunity exceeding $28 billion across AI-assisted trading, music production software, and online music education. McIvor is seeking seed capital of NZD $150,000–$250,000 to accelerate SongPal to beta, formalise the CoreeyAI API for B2B licensing, and launch the first MOSOKO education cohort.

Seven brands. One founder. One stack. Deployed and documented.
